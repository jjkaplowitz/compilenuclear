Joshua Kaplowitz

Founder & CEO

Josh Kaplowitz is a U.S. Naval Academy '22 graduate and nuclear-trained Submarine Officer in the U.S. Navy, where he has operated high-consequence nuclear propulsion plants, qualified to drive submarines, supervised reactor operations and teams of up to 70 sailors, and led in demanding shipyard and at-sea environments.

With a dual background in Computer Science and Nuclear Engineering, Josh combines rigorous technical expertise in reactor physics, radiological controls, electrical and mechanical systems with hands-on leadership in mission-critical settings. His Navy experience has involved supervising reactor plants, managing extreme stress under long hours, and driving continuous operational excellence—skills he's now channeling into building the next generation of advanced nuclear energy solutions.

Ryan Teuschl

Founder and CTO

Ryan T. Teuschl graduated 1st academically out of approximately 1200 students at the United States Naval Academy studying operational research. He excelled in the Navy’s elite nuclear pipeline, earning Highest Honors at Naval Nuclear Power School, topping his class at Prototype, and qualifying as a Nuclear Engineer at Naval Reactors Headquarters in Washington, DC—where he received formal recognition from the Director, Naval Reactors, for outstanding performance in the Prospective Nuclear Engineer Officer (PNEO) program.

As a Nuclear Submarine Officer and Submarine Warfare-qualified leader, Ryan accumulated extensive experience across four fast-attack submarines (three S6G and one S6W reactor plants). He played a key role in the decommissioning and defueling of USS Key West (SSN 722). His proven expertise in nuclear propulsion, reactor engineering, operational safety, and high-reliability leadership directly supports the development of advanced, safe, and scalable nuclear energy solutions.
